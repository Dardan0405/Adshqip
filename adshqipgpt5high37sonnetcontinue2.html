tag.js
<!-- Adshqip JS Tag - Asynchronous & GDPR ready -->
<script async src="https://cdn.adshqip.com/tag.js"></script>
<script>
  // Initialize with your site configuration
  adshqip.init({
    siteId: "YOUR_SITE_ID",
    formats: ["native", "interstitial", "push"],
    consent: true,  // GDPR-compliant consent handling
    language: "auto",  // Auto-detect or specify: "sq", "en"
    testMode: false,  // Set to true for testing
    onReady() {
      // Render ads when the tag is ready
      adshqip.render("#native-feed", { 
        feedId: "FEED_123", 
        items: 6,
        lazyLoad: true
      });
    }
  });
</script>

Frequently Asked About Pricing

Is there a minimum spend requirement?
The minimum campaign budget is $50. For CPA campaigns, minimums vary by vertical and conversion type. We recommend starting with at least $100 to gather enough data for optimization.
Can I switch pricing models mid-campaign?
Yes, you can switch between CPM and CPC anytime. Switching to CPA requires approval and usually a performance history with us. Your account manager can help with the transition to ensure uninterrupted campaigns.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes, we offer volume discounts starting at $1,000 monthly spend. For larger budgets over $5,000 monthly, we provide custom pricing packages with premium inventory access and dedicated support.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our platform and services.

Do you accept all verticals?

We accept most mainstream verticals with strict policies around adult, gambling, and sensitive content. All campaigns are reviewed before going live to ensure compliance with our guidelines and local regulations.

Prohibited verticals include illegal products/services, malware, discriminatory content, and misleading offers. If you're unsure about your vertical, please contact our support team for guidance.

How fast are payouts?

We offer Net7 and Net15 payment options, depending on your account tier and history. The minimum payout threshold varies by payment method:

  • PayPal: $20 minimum (processed within 24 hours)
  • Bank Transfer (SEPA/SWIFT): $50 minimum (1-3 business days)
  • Cryptocurrency: $20 minimum (processed within 24 hours)

For publishers with monthly earnings over $1,000, we offer custom payment schedules and reduced minimum thresholds.

Is there a WordPress plugin?

Yes, we offer a dedicated WordPress plugin called "Adshqip Ads" that you can install directly from your WP admin dashboard. The plugin features:

  • Easy configuration with your siteId
  • Format selection and placement controls
  • Auto-insertion options for content, sidebar, and footer
  • Lazy loading and performance optimization
  • Full AMP compatibility
  • GDPR/ePrivacy consent integration

The plugin is regularly updated and compatible with all major WordPress versions (5.0+).

What targeting options are available?

Our platform offers comprehensive targeting options to help you reach the right audience:

  • Geographic: Country, region, city, and postal code targeting
  • Device: OS, browser, screen size, and mobile carrier
  • Demographic: Language, age groups, and gender
  • Contextual: Website categories, keywords, and content topics
  • Behavioral: Interest segments and browsing patterns
  • Temporal: Day parting, frequency capping, and scheduling
  • Connection: ISP, connection type, and speed

For Albanian-specific campaigns, we offer unique targeting options like dialect regions and cultural interests relevant to the Balkan market.

How do you handle ad blocking?

We employ several strategies to minimize the impact of ad blockers:

  • Server-side ad rendering for critical formats
  • Proprietary ad-serving technology that avoids common blocking patterns
  • Multiple fallback delivery methods
  • Regular updates to our delivery code to stay ahead of blockers
  • Optional adblock detection and friendly messaging for publishers

Our system automatically detects ad blocking and adjusts delivery methods in real-time, helping publishers recover up to 30% of potentially lost revenue.
